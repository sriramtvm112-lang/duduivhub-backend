Our Story

Duduivhub is a startup founded by Sachin V, a college student who faced the same problems many students go through while planning industrial visits. From finding companies to managing travel, food, and stay — everything was a struggle. Seeing this gap, Sachin decided to solve it by creating Duduivhub — a one-stop solution for all industrial visit needs.

We arrange everything — industries to visit, hotels, food, and transport — at a reasonable and student-friendly cost. Our goal is simple: to make industrial visits easy, safe, and enjoyable for every student.
